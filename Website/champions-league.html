Big Decision

You are in the changing room about to play in the biggest game of your life. You walk out onto the field with the crowd going crazy. Your team gather for a photo before the game starts. The referee blows his whistle and it begins. The game is going by fast but out of no where, the opposition score. Your manager is telling yous to focus and play your own game. It seemed to work because not long later your team equalise. 1-1 at half time. The manager asks for more from yous in the second half and to make a name for yourself. In the second half there seems to be nothing happening from both team.. until. It's the 89th minute and you pick up the ball. You drive it forward and now have a decision to make that could change your life forever. Do you take a shot for goal and go for the glory. Pass the ball and hope your teammate scores. Or do you keep running with the ball and hope more space opens up for a better chance?
